# Camdao DAO Structure

Camdao uses a progressive decentralization model, expanding governance power as the ecosystem grows.

---

## Phase 1 — Advisory Governance (2025–2026)
- Core team sets direction
- Community feedback integrated via snapshots
- Token not yet controlling platform parameters

---

## Phase 2 — DAO Activation (Q4 2026)
Once $CD is launched and BitNaked is in public beta:
- Treasury decisions voted by token holders
- Creator incentive distribution proposals
- Parameter governance (fees, payouts, incentives)

---

## Phase 3 — Full DAO (2027+)
The DAO gains extended powers:
- Approve platform upgrades
- Select grant recipients
- Elect advisory members
- Manage treasury yield strategies
- Govern multi-chain expansion

---

## Governance Tools
- Snapshot-style voting (off-chain)
- On-chain proposals after audit
- Multi-sig treasury guardians
- Transparent financial reporting

---

## Token Holder Rights
- Propose ecosystem improvements
- Vote on changes to:
  - Fees
  - Rewards
  - Platform rules
  - Treasury spending
  - Creator grants
  - Feature priority
